Physical Hard Work vs. Mental Hard Work

Have you guys ever thought why a person owning/running the factory gets paid more than the people who are actually doing the work in factory?

The difference is simple the person running the factory is doing more mental hardwork than the physical hardwork if we compare them with the workers in the factory. Those workers are told what they have to do and all they have to do is do the physical work. Mental work is more hard and energy required than the physical work. The person with mental work is more responsible of things going right or wrong. The factory owner can do the physical work like the laboures of the factory but its not same with the laboures running the factory. Using your body to do work is easier than using your mind to do the work.

That's why the people running the companies are paid more even if they are just sitting in their office with ACs because they are running things and controlling stuff which if it goes wrong then they are to be blame.

If a person from the company does something wrong people will blame the owner of the company not that person who did the wrong.

Same goes for the PM or president of any country, they are not doing physical work, most of their work would be done by others for free but they are responsible for the mental decision they have to take. That's why a king is never fighting in a battle because he is the one taking decision for soldiers which is very tough thing to do if compared to fighting in a battle. The king is responsible for the state and his residents.

Thats why a labour working for 12 hours a day is not making as much money as a person sitting in office working for 12 hours for their company. Because the person is doing mental work which is lot tougher than the physical work.

That's why we humans are creating robots and AI to errode the physical work and then humans can focus more on the mental work which is absolute necessary for the state and humanity.
